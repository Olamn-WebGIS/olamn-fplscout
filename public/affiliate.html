Affiliate FAQ

How does affiliate commission work?
You earn ₦2,000 when a referred friend signs up and completes a valid premium subscription purchase.
How do I share my referral link?
Use the copy button or share buttons on this page to share your unique referral link with friends.
When can I withdraw earnings?
Earnings are available after the referral has completed a verified premium subscription. Withdrawals are submitted through the dashboard.
What is not allowed?
Self-referrals, duplicate accounts, fake signups, or any abusive behavior will result in forfeiture of commission and disqualification from the program.
Where can I view my referral activity?
The “Recent Referral History” section shows the latest referrals, status, and payout progress.
