Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I get started with FPL Scout?

Simply sign up on our website, choose a premium subscription, and start using all our advanced tools to enhance your FPL experience. Your first month gives you access to League Spy, Transfer Recommendations, and much more!

❓ Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription anytime. If you cancel before the end of your billing cycle, you'll retain access until your subscription expires. No questions asked. Check our Refund & Cancellation Policy for more details.

❓ Is there a free trial?

Currently, we don't offer a free trial, but our ₦3,000/month (~$3 USD) subscription is affordable and includes all premium features. You can cancel anytime within the first month if you're not satisfied.

❓ How accurate are your predictions?

Our predictive algorithm uses real-time data including player form, xG/xA statistics, and historical performance against specific opponents. While no prediction is 100% accurate, our users consistently see improved decision-making.

❓ Do you support payment in my country?

We accept payments in NGN (Nigerian Naira) and USD. Payment is processed through our secure payment gateway. If you have issues, please contact us at info@fplscout.name.ng.

❓ What is League Spy?

League Spy is our premium tool that lets you track all managers in your mini-leagues in real-time. See their transfers, captain choices, chip usage, and bench decisions to spot opportunities and stay ahead of your rivals.
